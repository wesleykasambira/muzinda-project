First Class

First class is a travel class on some passenger airliners intended to be more luxurious than business class, premium economy, and economy class. On a passenger jetliner, first class refers to a limited number (rarely more than 20) of seats or cabins toward the front of the aircraft which have more space, comfort, service, and privacy.

Business Class

Business class is a travel class available on many commercial minded people who want to work during their travel. It was originally intended as an intermediate level of service between economy class and first class, having eliminated first class seating. Business class is distinguished from other travel classes by the quality of seating, food, drinks, ground service and other amenities.

Premium Economy

Premium Economy class, is slightly above Economy Class in terms of services provided. Is the second lowest travel class of seating in air travel. In summary everything provided in Premium Economy is 20% above Economy Class and the prices do not differ much.

Economy Class

Economy class, also called coach class, steerage, or standard class, is the lowest travel class of seating in air travel. Economy class seats usually recline and have a fold-down table.A pocket on the seat in front will contain an airsickness bag, inflight magazine, Duty-Free catalogue and a safety and evacuation card.Availability of food varies too. Meals are now only generally provided on international flights.

Children
